
Hardware Abstraction Layer (HAL) from ST Microelectronics

Currently the version 1.2.5 of the STM32G4xx HAL is used.
https://github.com/STMicroelectronics/stm32g4xx-hal-driver/tree/v1.2.5

Currently the version 1.4.6 of the STM32G0xx HAL is used.
https://github.com/STMicroelectronics/stm32g0xx-hal-driver/tree/v1.4.6
